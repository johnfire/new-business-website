Smile


when she smiles you can see inside her soul

deep inside

you can see the passion and joy for life

and the power

the power that only a few brave souls have

to be themselves, and damn what the world thinks


I can see her spirit

and it is so full of love and passion

and also some fear

but the love and passion

clearly overrule the later


she smiles not only with her mouth

but also with her eyes

and that kind of smile

is like no other

because it holds a endless well of thought and feelings

drowned in passion


I want to know that smile

and I hope she wants to know mine.
