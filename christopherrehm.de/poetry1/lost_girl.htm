The lost girl

 

She’s a lost Girl

No matter how cool she looks

All the money in the world

Cannot change her hook

And she’s  losing

More of her soul all the time

 

 

She’s a lost girl

A fake cultural pearl

She a lost girl

Lost in her own world

Lost girl

Lost forever

 

Wandering the world

Searching for a thrill

While missing the meaning

Of real love still

It’s everyone else’s fault

And never hers

Cause she never looks inside herself

To see what she could cure

 

Wandering the world still

Still seeking that cheap thrill

A real love is nowhere around

Getting lonelier as she gets older

Cannot solve the equation

Until she adds in her own dimension

Today she is lonelier

Cause she is still a lost girl

 

 

And I feel the pain having watched her all these years.
