Faith

 

It is faith that makes love real

and holds us twined together

bound as one in happieness

it is faith that glues it all as one

Faith deep inside our souls

deep down inside

 

dont let it die

or fly away

for once it does

the end is near

hold it close

its very dear

 

build faith one day by day

never let it go away

once it does the love will die

and pass to ashes

dust, tumbleweed dry.
