My Portfolio

A collection of websites and projects I have worked on, these were all built using Claude Code, and the Sonnet 4.6 or Opus models.

  • Flashkarte A web and Android app that allows the user to create decks of flashcards and use them for spaced repetition learning.
  • Notes World A note taking app that runs on the web and on your Android phone.
  • LeGuilde A website that allows artists to set up their portfolio on the web for public viewing and contact with their fans. There are also two Android apps: one for artists to manage their portfolio, and one for collectors and fans to look at artists' works and contact the artists directly.
  • General CRM A general customer relationship management and lead finding system of agents for marketing projects.
